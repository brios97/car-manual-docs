Choke

The choke enriches the petrol mixture, and is used to start the car in cold weather or when the engine is cold.

The choke is the knob on the right of the control panel marked 'C'.

When the choke knob is pulled, it gives a progressively richer mixture of air and petrol to the engine.

The choke will hold in any position.

Depending on the weather, the choke knob should be pushed fully in or in to the 'weak' position as soon as the engine has warmed up.

  • In cold weather, the operator should wait for the engine to run for several minutes with the choke pushed in to the 'weak' position.
  • In warm weather, the operator should push the choke control knob fully in almost immediately.
