Cleaning the Interior

Brush or vaccuum the interior upholstery of the car. Make sure to use the right tools.

Clean the interior before washing the outside of the vehicle.

  • Clean the carpets with a stiff brush or vacuum cleaner.
  • ApplyBMCA 2-way Cleaning Fluid to the carpets with a soft brush.
  • Apply BMCA 2-way Cleaning Fluid to the upholstery and and roof lining with a damp cloth using a light rubbing action.
  • Use a razor blade to remove transfers from the window glass.
