Introduction

This manual will help a new operator understand the operation and maintenance of his vehicle so that he can enjoy many hours of driving pleasure.

Welcome to the growing family of Mini owners. This vehicle has been delivered with confidence.

This manual provides all the information necessary to maintain the vehicle in first-class condition and to ensure trouble-free service.

Operators should read this manual before operating the vehicle.

Reading this manual

This manual includes information for the Mini Sedan, Mini Countryman and Mini Traveller models.

All information, specifications and illustrations in this manual are correct at the time of printing.

Detailed and technical information is contained in the Workshop Manual which is available from any authorised Mini dealer.
