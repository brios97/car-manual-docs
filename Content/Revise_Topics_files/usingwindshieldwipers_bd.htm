Using windshield wipers

The windshield wipers have rubber blades which wipe back and forth across the windshield, keeping rain and other precipitation from blocking the driver's vision.

The windshield wiper switch is located on the left of the ignition switch.

Tip: The windshield wipers only function when the ignition is switched on.

  1. Move the switch downwards to turn the wipers on.
  2. Note: Wait until the wipers are at the end of the stroke, horizontal (parallel with the bonnet) before switching off.
    Move the switch upwards to turn off the wipers.
