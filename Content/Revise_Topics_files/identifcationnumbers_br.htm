Identification Numbers

Unique identification numbers are stamped on the car, the engine and the transmission case. They are used to identify your particular car.

 

Location of car part numbers
Car part Number location
Car Number Find the number on a plate located between the radiator and the left fender.
Engine Number Look for the number on the metal plate fixed to the right side of the cylinder block.
Transmission Casing Assembly Number Check the casing just below the starter motor for the number's location.
