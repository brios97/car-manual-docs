Opening the hood

Open the hood to access the engine. It has two separate mechanisms to ensure it is securely fastened.

Note: When the hood is open, ensure the release lever and safety catch are well lubricated.
  1. Insert a finger into the center louver (vent) at the top of the radiator shell.
  2. Move the release lever toward the right-hand side of the vehicle.
  3. Push down on the hood with your other hand to assist movement of the lever.
    Note: The hood is released but is still held by the safety catch.
  4. Push the safety catch inwards and raise the hood.
  5. Hold the hood open with one hand.
  6. With your other hand, remove the prop rod from the rubber clip on the right side.
  7. Secure the prop rod into the support bracket on the right-hand side of the engine bay.
