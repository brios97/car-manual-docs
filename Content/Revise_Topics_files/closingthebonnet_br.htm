Closing the hood

 

  1. Raise the hood slightly to remove the prop.
  2. Place the prop back in its clip.
  3. Lower the hood to engage the safety catch.
  4. Apply hand pressure to press the hood fully closed.
    Note: The hood is fully closed when you hear the safety catch and hood lock engage.
    WARNING: ENSURE THE HOOD IS SECURELY FASTENED TO PREVENT FROM OPENING WHILE DRIVING, WHICH COULD BLOCK YOUR VIEW AND CAUSE AN ACCIDENT.
